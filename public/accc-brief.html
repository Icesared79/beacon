The Problem

Families don't ask for help until it's too late

Most families in financial distress don't contact credit counseling until they've already missed payments, received foreclosure notices, or exhausted their savings. By then, the options are limited and the damage is done. ACCC counselors are highly effective — but they can only help people who walk through the door.

What Beacon Does

Find the families who need us before they know to ask

Beacon ingests public records, government data, and economic indicators to build a real-time picture of household financial distress — then surfaces the families who would most benefit from ACCC's counseling services.

📊

Household Intelligence

Every identified household gets a distress profile built from 27+ public signals — mortgage status, tax delinquency, court filings, employment trends, and more.

🔴

Distress Scoring

Beacon assigns severity levels (Critical, High, Moderate, Low) so counselors can prioritize outreach to families closest to crisis.

📍

Geographic Coverage

Coverage maps show where Beacon has intelligence and where gaps exist — helping ACCC expand outreach strategically across service areas.

⚡

Proactive Outreach

Instead of waiting for families to call, counselors can identify at-risk households in their zip codes and reach out before financial collapse.

Technical Architecture

Built on Atlas — 93 million records and growing

Beacon is powered by Atlas, Red Planet Data's core intelligence engine. Atlas ingests data from 500+ government sources, normalizes it at the parcel level, and generates composite distress scores. Beacon is a purpose-built interface that translates Atlas intelligence into counselor-ready workflows for ACCC.

Built and operated by Red Planet Data exclusively for American Consumer Credit Counseling.
