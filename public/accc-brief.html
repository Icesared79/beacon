About Beacon

Beacon is a homeowner intelligence platform built on Atlas, Red Planet Data's national property data engine. It surfaces households showing signs of financial distress — HMDA loan denials, tax delinquency, high vacancy, and equity signals — so counselors have a clear picture of who needs help and why.

The platform is organized around the counselor's workflow: review a household, read the outreach briefing, look up contact information, and flag for counseling. All data flows from Atlas — no manual data entry required.

How It Works
1

Browse the Households list

Records are grouped by distress level — High Need and Monitor. Each row shows the owner name, address, primary distress signal, assessed value, and how long the signal has been active. Filters let you narrow by state, signal type, or equity range.

2

Open a household record

The detail page shows full property information, distress indicators with plain-English explanations, an outreach briefing summarizing the situation, and the property's assessed value and sale history where available.

3

Look up contact information and flag

The Look Up Contact button retrieves phone numbers and email addresses via skip trace. From there, flag the household for counseling, assign it to a counselor, and add notes. The activity log tracks all actions taken.
